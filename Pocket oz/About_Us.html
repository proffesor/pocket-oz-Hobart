As well as providing information about the specific location on which this App is focused, your Pocket-Oz App also provides access to the Australia For Everyone network of travel and leisure websites, which cover all states and territories, capital cities and regional centres. All websites to which this App links are fully mobile friendly. Though each website has its own web address, your can pass from one website to another via the Destinations page of your Pocket-Oz App. So wherever you go in Australia, you have instant access to everything you need to know about where you are via this App.
